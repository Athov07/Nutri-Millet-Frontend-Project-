Foxtail Rava Dosa

Ingredients:

EatMillet Foxtail idli rava 1 cup, Wheat flour 1 cup, Medium size onion -1, Chilli-1, Coriander leaves, Salt as per your taste.

  • Making process:

  • Take 1 cup of EatMillet foxtail idli rava and what flour 1 cup and mix it well
  • Add onions and chillies and coriander leaves then mix it
  • And add some sufficient water to make like dosa batter, mix it well
  • Take a pan and some oil on pan and add sufficient batter on the pan and fry it both sides
  • Then ready to serve the EatMillet foxtail idli rava dosa with tomato chutney Or ground nut chutney.

Foxtail Millet Pongal

Ingredients:

1/2 cup EatMillet Foxtail Millet Upma rava , 1/2 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split), 2 Green Chillies , 1 Tsp Cumin seeds (Jeera), 2 Tsp Whole Black Peppercorns , 1 sprig Curry leaves, 8 Cashew nuts , 1/4 Tsp Asafoetida (hing) , 1 Tsp Ginger , chopped , 2 Tsp Ghee 1 Tsp Sunflower Oil, Salt to taste.

  • Making process:

  • First dry roast foxtail millet Upma Rava and moong dal in a hot kadai. This will bring a nice aroma to the Pongal.
  • Wash & soak roasted foxtail millet Upma Rava and moong dal together for about 1 hour. Soaking helps in faster cooking.
  • In a pressure cooker, add the soaked EatMillet Foxtail millet Upma rava, Dal, 4 cups of water, whole green chilies and pressure cook for 3-4 whistles or until soft.
  • Once pressure is released, remove the cooker lid and mash the mixture using a ladle.
  • In a deep bottom pan, add ghee and roast cashews in hot ghee until they turn golden brown. Remove cashews and keep them aside.
  • Into the same pan, add oil, once hot, add cumin seeds, peppercorns, asafoetida, salt, chopped ginger and curry leaves. Sauté for a minute or two. Now add cooked Eat Millet foxtail millet – Upma rava and mix it well making sure no lumps are formed.Reduce the flame and cook it for 5 minutes.
  • Serve the EatMillet Foxtail Millet Upma rava Pongal hot with red chilli coconut chutney and Mixed Vegetable Sambar or even spicy lemon pickle goes well with Pongal.

Foxtail Millet Mango Rice

Ingredients:

Foxtail millet: 1 cup,water: 2 cups, Raw mango, Grated 1 or per taste, Groundnuts 2 tsp, Seasoning curry leaves, Chillies, Mustard seeds, Blackgram dal, Turmeric, Asafoetida, oil, salt to taste.

  • Making process:

  • Cook the millet in water and let it cool before mixing the rest of the ingredients. Fry groundnuts in oil, keep aside. Prepare the seasoning.
  • Add grated mango and saute for a minute. Add the cooked millet and mix Tangy mango, Rice is ready….. serve hot.

Millet Modak

Ingredients:

Eat Millet Idli rava – 1 cup, Palm jaggery – 1 cup Water – 1 cup , Ghee – 2 tsp, Coconut – 2 tbsp (chopped), Cashew nuts – 2 tbsp (chopped), Green cardamom powder – 1 tsp, Salt – 1 pinch

  • Making process:

  • Take one cup of Eat Millet idli rava And clean it and Roast it for 10 to 15 mints
  • Take 1 cup of water and add 1 cup palm jaggery. let the jaggery get dissolved
  • Then take a kadai and add 1 tablespoon of ghee and add 2 tablespoon of chopped coconut and 2 tablespoons cashew nuts then roast them.
  • Then strain the Jaggery water with the strainer and add green cardamom powder 1 tsp and once it start boiling switch of flame
  • Then now add Eat millet idli rava little by little into it and make sure no lumps are formed using a whisk helps in this then now switch on the stove Mix it well and add 1 tsp of ghee into it mix it well
  • Now add 1 pinch salt then mix it well till the batter becomes like a dough let it cool it then apply little ghee in to your hand and make the dumplings and serve it.
